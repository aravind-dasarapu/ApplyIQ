# Job Description: Senior ML Engineer - Fintech Solutions

**Company:** QuantFlow Systems
**Industry:** Financial Technology / Streaming Data

### Role Overview
We are looking for a Senior ML Engineer to architect high-frequency trading signal models and build RAG-based systems for financial news extraction.

### Core Requirements
* **Production-Grade LLMs:** Experience fine-tuning Llama-3 or Mistral for domain-specific tasks.
* **Vector Databases:** Expert-level knowledge of **Weaviate** or **Milvus** for high-performance RAG pipelines.
* **Stream Processing:** Proven experience with **Apache Kafka** or **Flink** for real-time feature engineering.
* **Cloud & MLOps:** Hands-on experience with **Kubernetes (K8s)**, **GitHub Actions** for CI/CD, and **AWS SageMaker**.
* **Advanced Mathematics:** Strong background in Bayesian statistics and Reinforcement Learning (RL).

### Technical Stack
* Python (Cython/PyPy preferred for performance).
* PyTorch, Ray, HuggingFace Transformers.
* PostgreSQL, Redis, Weaviate.
* AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, Terraform.
